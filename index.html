Projects

Projects

Algosys

Created by HackBreakers, AlgoSys transforms the way students learn Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) through interactive visualizations. Struggling with complex DSA concepts ourselves, we built this platform to provide a clear and engaging learning experience. AlgoSys features a code editor, an algorithm repository, and an MCQ quiz section, making it a complete e-lab for mastering DSA. With step-by-step execution and real-time coding, it bridges the gap between theory and implementation, turning abstract logic into intuitive learning.

LearnNex

LearnNex is our first team project, and we proudly won our college hackathon with it. Designed for 1st to 5th standard students, this interactive platform makes learning engaging and effective. We provide educational videos, gamified quizzes, subject-related games, and structured notes to enhance the learning experience. The platform also includes a track-based study system, allowing students to follow a structured learning path. Additionally, parental controls help monitor progress, while a canvas feature encourages creativity. With LearnNex, we aim to make early education more interactive, enjoyable, and well-structured.

RAN Store

RAN Store is my first individual project—an e-commerce platform designed for seamless online book shopping. Built with a user-friendly interface, it offers a smooth and engaging experience for book lovers. With an extensive collection, easy navigation, and a secure checkout process, RAN Store makes book purchasing simple and hassle-free. The platform features personalized recommendations, category-based browsing, and a wishlist option, ensuring that users find the right books effortlessly. Designed for accessibility and efficiency, RAN Store transforms online book shopping into a convenient and enjoyable experience
